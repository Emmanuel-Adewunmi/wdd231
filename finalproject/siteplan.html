Site Purpose

The purpose of RazerBud is to provide users with a simple yet powerful tool for tracking their income and expenses, visualizing spending habits, and setting financial goals. The site will allow users to add transactions, categorize expenses, view summaries and insights, and store preferences locally for a personalized experience.

Color Schema

The selected color palette gives RazerBud a modern, tech-forward look with strong contrast for readability and accessibility.

  • Charcoal Gray (#1C1C1C): Used for headers, navigation bar, and background accents to convey stability and sophistication.
  • Teal (#009688): Used for buttons, highlights, and interactive elements to provide a fresh, modern, and energetic feel.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for body text backgrounds for strong readability against dark elements.
